FAQ

How do I start a race?

Open DaisyGT, select your vehicle, choose a race mode, and invite opponents or find open races.

How do I sell NFTs?

Go to the Marketplace from any app, mint your race/hunt data as an NFT, and list it for sale.

Is DaisyAI always listening?

Only if you enable Autonomous Mode in Settings. Otherwise, DaisyAI responds to the wake word.
